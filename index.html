About the artist

My name is Fenia Chatzimichail and I am a graduate student of Athens School of Fine Arts. Currently, I am a postgraduate student in the interdisciplinary program of "Art and Public Sphere" at the Aristoteile University of Thessaloniki. I have been involved in education for several years, both in the public and private sectors. I have a deep passion for design, particularly object design with a functional character. Over the years, I have extensively practiced the linoleum technique, also known as block printing.

I am the founder of "Great Greek Love", which focuses on the design of souvenirs and oracle objects inspired by Greek culture. This project is divided into smaller ones, each drawing inspiration from specific aspects of Greek heritage, such as Minoan frescoes or Ancient Toys.

Great Greek Love aims to capture the essence of Greek civilization and convert it into unique and meaningful designs. Through the block printing technique, each work of Great Greek Love celebrates the richness and beauty of Greek Culture.
